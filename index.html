About Rajadarshini
Bharatanatyam, Yoga & the Architecture of Moving Bodies

Rajadarshini Saravanan is an internationally acclaimed Bharatanatyam dancer, yoga practitioner, and educator with over two decades of experience across India, Europe, Asia, the UAE and the UK. Trained under Dr. Smt. Sheela Unnikrishnan at Sridevi Nrithyalaya, she has performed in more than 300 productions worldwide and mentors students through a practice that bridges tradition, science and mindfulness.

Disciple of Dr. Smt. Sheela Unnikrishnan 20+ years of intensive training International performances & teaching

Awards, Roles & Grants
  • Natwar Gopikrishna National Award (2025) – for Bharatanatyam by AIDA International, supported by the Ministry of Culture, Government of India.
  • Founder, TAT Yoga Studio – offering online and in-person training to students across India, Europe, Asia and the UK.
  • Faculty (2013–2022), Sridevi Nrithyalaya – mentoring students and assisting in large-scale choreographic works.

Practice & Pedagogy
Natyoga Darshanam – Movement Approach

Through her practice framework "Natyoga Darshanam", Rajadarshini weaves Bharatanatyam grammar, yoga lineages and somatic awareness into an integrated movement language. Technique, breath, rhythm and stillness are explored as one continuum – enabling dancers, movers and yoga practitioners to cultivate strength, clarity and expressive depth.
