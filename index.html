About Us
Natyoga Darshanam
Integrative movement discipline
Yoga (union and awareness) + Natya (dance and expression)

Natyoga Darshanam unites the ancient sciences of Yoga and Natya into a holistic pathway of embodied transformation. Rooted in the Yogasutra of Sage Patanjali and the Natyashastra of Bharatamuni, it is not merely a dance pedagogy or a yoga system — it is a transformative approach that trains the nervous system, refines expressive depth, and restores joy in artistic embodiment. Founded by Rajadarshini Saravanan — Bharatanatyam dancer, yoga practitioner, movement researcher & educator — to promote preservation, education, research, and the collective upliftment of the artistic community beyond race.

Vision

To establish Natyoga Darshanam as a globally recognised integrative movement institution that harmonises yoga, classical dance, and embodied awareness, nurturing injury-free, emotionally intelligent, and spiritually aligned dancers who experience stillness within dynamic movement.

Natyoga Darshanam was founded to promote preservation, education, research, and the collective upliftment of the artistic community beyond race.

Mission
  • To create a holistic training methodology combining Yoga, Nritta, and Abhinaya
  • To promote conscious biomechanics and breath-integrated movement
  • To prevent and rehabilitate physical and psychological injuries in dancers
  • To develop expressive authenticity rooted in bhava and self-awareness
  • To offer a research-driven and tradition-rooted movement education system
Objectives
  • To promote and propagate integrative movement education combining Yoga and Indian classical dance.
  • To conduct training programs, workshops, retreats, and certification courses.
  • To research injury prevention and rehabilitation methods for performing artists.
  • To promote emotional and psychological well-being through embodied arts.
  • To establish centers for holistic dance education.
  • To collaborate with national and international institutions.
  • To publish educational material and research in the field of movement sciences.
  • To provide scholarships and outreach programs for underprivileged artists.

Practice & Pedagogy
Natyoga Darshanam – Movement Approach

Through her practice framework "Natyoga Darshanam", Rajadarshini weaves Bharatanatyam grammar, yoga lineages and somatic awareness into an integrated movement language. Technique, breath, rhythm and stillness are explored as one continuum – enabling dancers, movers and yoga practitioners to cultivate strength, clarity and expressive depth.
