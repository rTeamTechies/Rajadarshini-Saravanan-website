About Us
Natyoga Darshanam
Integrative movement discipline
Yoga (union and awareness) + Natya (dance and expression)

Natyoga Darshanam unites the ancient sciences of Yoga and Natya into a holistic pathway of embodied transformation. Rooted in the Yogasutra of Sage Patanjali and the Natyashastra of Bharatamuni, it is not merely a dance pedagogy or a yoga system — it is a transformative approach that trains the nervous system, refines expressive depth, and restores joy in artistic embodiment.

Founder
Rajadarshini Saravanan

Bharatanatyam artist, yoga practitioner, movement researcher and educator with 20+ years of performance and teaching experience across India, Europe, Asia, the UAE and the United Kingdom. Vision · Objectives

Rooted intention for dancers & artistic communities

Vision

To establish Natyoga Darshanam as a globally recognised integrative movement institution that harmonises yoga, classical dance, and embodied awareness, nurturing injury-free, emotionally intelligent, and spiritually aligned dancers who experience stillness within dynamic movement.

Objectives
  • Research injury prevention and rehabilitation methods for performing artists.
  • Publish educational material and movement research for dancers and teachers.
  • Offer scholarships and outreach programs for underrepresented artistic communities.
